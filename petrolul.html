Petrolul Ploiesti

Fotbal Club Petrolul Ploiesti is a Romanian professional football club based in Ploiesti currently playing in the Liga I. The club is ranked 5th in the Liga I All-Time Table, with 55 seasons played to this date. The team's colors are yellow and blue.
