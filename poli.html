Politehnica Timisoara

Fotbal Club Politehnica Timisoara, commonly referred to as Poli Timisoara, was a Romanian football club established in 1921 and dissolved in September 2012. The team had won two Romanian Cups and was twice a runner-up in Liga I. It's ranked 8th in the Liga I All-Time Table, with 49 seasons played.
